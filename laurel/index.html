Welcome to Laurel Roller Skating Center!

MASK POLICY CHANGE: MASKS ARE NOW REQUIRED even on Skating Floor.

  • Due to circumstances we will now be charging a $2.00 spectator fee to non-skaters.
  • If you are using rental skates you must wear crew sock or knee socks (no ankle socks). Rental Skates are disinfected before and after each use.
  • The Skating Center is following all the guidelines provided by the CDC for cleaning and sanitizing the Skating Center.
  • We have added new signs with information about COVID-19, we have added several hand sanitizing stations and have done some remodeling to enable us to keep the highly touched surfaces clean and sanitized.
  • We are allowing use of "skate mates" during the afternoon sessions only. They will be disinfected before and after use.
